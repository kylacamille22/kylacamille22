My experience in Sqaurespace

I've heard a lot about Squarespace, as it's been mentioned quite a bit in most social media platforms. I really like working in Sqaurespace more than WordPress. The templates available are more on my style and aethetically pleasing. I think Squarespace is way better when doing online selling and personal blog is probably best for WordPress. The website was really easy to use and I didn't have to code anything, so It didn't took a long time to create.
